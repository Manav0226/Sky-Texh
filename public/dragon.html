Merlin is a family of rocket engines developed by SpaceX for use on its Falcon 1,

Falcon 9 and Falcon Heavy launch vehicles. Merlin engines use a rocket grade kerosene (RP-1)

The Merlin engine was originally designed for recovery and reuse.
